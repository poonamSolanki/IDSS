Welcome to Investment Support System

The Investment Support App Web Portal is a comprehensive platform designed to help users manage their investment domains and overall financial management effectively. Users can categorize their investments into various domains and subcategories for detailed tracking. The portal also supports expense management by allowing users to log expenses, manage expense factors, and categorize expenses for detailed analysis.
